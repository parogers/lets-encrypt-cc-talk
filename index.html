What is Let's Encrypt?

Let's Encrypt is a certificate authority that grants free X.509 certificates via an easy-to-use, automated process.

What are digital certificates?

  • X.509 certificates underpin the security of the modern internet (HTTPS)

What is certificate authority?

  • Manages digital certificates (called X.509)
  • Provides a sense of trust
